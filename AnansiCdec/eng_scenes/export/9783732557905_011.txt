Sudden honking and squealing tires made her flinch. She almost lost her balance. Heavens, had the truck driver gone crazy? There was a loud bang as he crashed into the passenger car next to her. In the next moment, Lorena was hit by it and thrown to the side. She landed on her stomach on the curb. A searing pain jolted through her chest. The last thing she perceived was a young man with a hat on his curly hair, who came rushing out of his car with a horrified expression. Then a merciful faintness relieved her of her pain.
