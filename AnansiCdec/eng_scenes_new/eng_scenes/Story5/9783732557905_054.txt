In the evening, Lorena immersed herself in the script that Siggi had sent her in the meantime. It was a very touching Christmas story, and after the first few pages, Lorena's mind was made up. Yes, she would do the play. The new date Siggi had chosen also suited her, because she had no other commitments that day. She would somehow be able to squeeze in the rehearsals with Dr. Krug. Lorena had to think of Theo again. How nice it would have been if she had still had contact with him and he could have watched her play. In the Elisabeth Hospital, where everything had started and ended much too quickly...
