 He now made the whole servants rebel. Darkness had descended in the meantime. The servants had to equip themselves with torches and lanterns and were to search the park and the forest. He himself joined them. It had become a certainty to him that something had happened to Griseldis, and his restlessness increased to the point of despair. For hours they searched the forest for Griseldis without finding any trace of her. Harro returned with the servants shortly before midnight. He looked pale and miserable to pity.
