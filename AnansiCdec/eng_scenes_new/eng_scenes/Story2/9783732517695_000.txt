Lost in thought, Stella gazes into the flames of the fireplace. Ever since she and Patrick have been going in and out of Windhome Castle, she has had a growing feeling that something is not right here. The duke and his mother are asking a conspicuous number of questions about Patrick's background. Also strange is what Stella has learned about the Youngs' family chronicle. It says there that a great misfortune always happens at the castle when a storm comes up. Shivering, Stella nestles into Patrick's arms. She senses more and more clearly that her beloved husband is in danger, but she cannot explain why. Then suddenly the fire in the fireplace begins to flicker restlessly, doors creak, and the branches of the old trees outside bang against the windows. "A storm is coming," Stella whispers, startled ...
