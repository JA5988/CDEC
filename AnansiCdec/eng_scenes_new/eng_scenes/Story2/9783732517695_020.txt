Little Emily must have gotten the castellan all on her side, because the very next day she visited Stella and Patrick, and again the day after next. She stayed for an hour. Later Stella said to Patrick: "Doesn't it hurt you to see how little Emily is satisfied with? Just one stolen hour makes her happy; the rest of the day she's on her own." "There are plenty of kids around, aren't there," Patrick countered. "They would all make good playmates for Emily." "Duchess Alice doesn't allow any of the children to come to the castle. I don't think the children want anything to do with Emily either, because the Duchess demands that they call her Lady Emily.
