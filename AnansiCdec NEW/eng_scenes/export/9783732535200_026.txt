Boris Corman's excitement had not diminished in the course of the last half hour, but had even increased. The news that not only parts of prehistory had been transferred to the present, but that it was apparently also possible to travel to the past in this way unharmed, had really electrified him. Until now, he had only produced small amounts of his serum, since he had assumed that he would only need it for the area of DINO-LAND. Instead, global perspectives suddenly opened up to him. He would wipe out all the dinosaurs of prehistory at once!
