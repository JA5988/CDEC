 When they reached the underground lagoon, the next shock came. The Hydrites, who had decided against the liberation operation, had returned to the cave. "What are you doing here? Why didn't you escape as we discussed?" asked Guan'ril audibly stunned. "The battle is over. We may have won, but the soldiers will follow us. This is no longer a safe place!" "Neither is the sea," replied a Hydrit from among the returnees. "You spoke of whirlpools. Of a few unusual rapids that have appeared along the coast since the world swap. But the truth is that just like on land, an impenetrable barrier has appeared in the water. Algae that normally grows only a few feet high has proliferated to the water's surface and beyond, coalescing into a solid wall that none of our knives could penetrate. We are trapped!"
