When Gwendolin was expecting a child, Duke Brian changed his will. His wife had achieved her goal; her son would be the heir to Windhome. Young Duke Patrick had lost his rights. All her moves had worked out. One thing, however, she did not know: how often her husband rode restlessly across the fields, that late in the evening he was still standing at the tomb where Patrick's mother was buried. But Duke Brian no longer had the strength to resist his wife and give in to those voices that warned him about her and Robert. The two of them had done too well. Still Duke Brian believed in the outrages that his son Patrick should have committed.The man, so quickly aged and tired, was ashamed of the longing for his son....
