 *** Nobody knew who had given the name Windhome to the castle of the Dukes Young. Even in the family chronicle one searched for it in vain. House of the Wind - it was a modest name compared to that of other castles in Ireland. But also the Youngs of Windhome had always been modest. The sons of the Youngs had been honest fighters for their fatherland, clergymen, scientists and lords of the manor.
