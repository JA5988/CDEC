When they then entered the dining room, Rosemarie also appeared together with Frau Ellen. Rosemarie had meanwhile composed herself. She still looked a little pale, of course, but beautiful. The meal passed without any disturbance. Rosemarie and the two brothers made an effort to appear impartial, eagerly grasping at every topic so as not to allow a break in the conversation. Mrs. Ellen, at any rate, remained clueless. After table, the gentlemen retired to go about their business.
